Muslim Matching

A modern matrimony app that helps practising Muslims find a spouse the halal way. Built around serious marriage intentions, guardian (wali) involvement, and privacy by default — no swiping for sport, no exposure of your photos to the whole internet.

Members create a considered profile, set clear preferences, and connect respectfully with the option to involve a guardian from the start. Designed to feel calm, safe, and purposeful.
